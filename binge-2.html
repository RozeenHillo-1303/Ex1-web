HERCAI



Drama series, Romantic Turkish, the first part of which was shown on March 15,2019 on the ITV channel,
and ended on May 31, 2019 starring Akin Akinoso, Ebru Shaheen,Gulchen Sentryoglu,Serhat Tutumluer,
Oia Onustasi, Ahmed Tansu Tashanlar, Sardar Ozir,Gulchen Khatkhan Eli Arkok, Jahid Jok,
Aida Axel and Majid Sonkan, written by Ida Tazjan and Vahriyah Shaheen,
directed by Jim Carrey and Binal Tairi.
